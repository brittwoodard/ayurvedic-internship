Application for Ayurvedic Internship Retreat

Thank you for submitting your application for the Ayurvedic Internship Program.

Here's what to expect next:

  • Review Process: Your application will be reviewed by our Preceptor to determine eligibility for the program. This process should take approximately 7 business days.
  • Contract & Reservation: Upon approval, a contract will be sent to your provided email address, along with our bank details for the money transfer.
  • Securing Your Spot: To reserve your place in the internship, please complete the contract and make a $500 USD non-refundable, non-transferable deposit.
  • Minimum Participation: A minimum of 5 interns is required for the retreat to proceed. Prompt reservation is essential.
  • Payment Plan Deadline: For those interested in the 3-month payment plan, the $500 deposit must be completed by February 1, 2024.

We're excited about your interest in our program and look forward to potentially working with you!
